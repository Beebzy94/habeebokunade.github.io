BIOGRAPHY

This is a counselling therapy business based in the USA. They are focused on improving some aspects of their clients' lives through different types of counseling.

The purpose of this logo is to give the company an identity so that it can grow the business by capturing new clients who build trust with the organization. In the long run, clients build a strong relationship with a brand that has a strong identity that can be easily familiarized with.

The aim was to create a strong combination mark with text and the use of a mountain vector image. The letter A was replaced with the mountain to form a strong combination mark that represents what the client was looking to achieve.
